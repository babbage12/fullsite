# Derrick Hudson - Official Website

The official website for Derrick Hudson, a professional musician and entertainer based in Toledo, Ohio.

## About

This is the personal website and portfolio for Derrick Hudson, featuring:

- **Live Music Services** - Solo guitar/vocalist and pianist available for venues, events, and private functions
- **Biography** - 12 years of experience performing on luxury cruise lines worldwide
- **Shiplife Experience** - Link to the interactive 3D globe documenting life at sea
- **Video Gallery** - Performance clips and highlights
- **Booking Information** - Contact for availability and rates

## Career Highlights

- 3 years as soloist with Seabourn (rated #1 small cruise line in the world)
- Queen's Room Vocalist on the iconic Queen Victoria (Cunard)
- Performances with Holland America and P&O Australia cruise lines
- Resident musician at Reithi Rah Resort in the Maldives
- Currently performing throughout NW Ohio and surrounding areas
