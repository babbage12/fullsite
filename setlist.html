About My Performance

Welcome! I'm a solo loop artist creating full-band experiences using my RC-600 looper, guitar with bass pickup, and HandSonic drum machine. Each song you see below is arranged and performed live, building layers in real-time to recreate the magic of these timeless classics. From intimate jazz standards to driving rock anthems, I craft dynamic performances that honor the originals while showcasing the unique possibilities of live looping.
